Form Leads — Quoted + Cubic Feet

Sync the quoted and cubic_feet columns of Form Leads from the Granot Follow Up Estimates table into Vantage. Scan the page, review syncable rows, then sync individually, in bulk, or on a schedule via ScanAndSync.

Auto-syncing

Actions

ScanAndSync (auto loop)

How ScanAndSync works (click to expand)

Every interval, the loop will:

  1. Re-scan the Granot Follow Up Estimates table on the active tab.
  2. For each row with a valid ref_no AND prior of 0 or 1:
    • Look up the current Form Lead in Vantage.
    • Compare its quoted and cubic_feet.
    • If they differ, PATCH the Form Lead.
  3. Rows with invalid/missing ref_no, missing prior, or prior > 1 are skipped.
  4. Booked jobs (prior = 5) are not in this table and are never touched.
  5. Errors do not stop the loop — they appear in the cycle history below.

This is equivalent to clicking Sync All Supported once per interval.

⚠ Manual selection is paused while ScanAndSync is running. Stop the loop above to scan and sync rows by hand again.

Call Leads — Match by Phone & Enrich

When a CSR creates a Call Lead in the CRM and the customer's phone matches a Follow Up Estimates row, sync job_no, cubic_feet, pickup & delivery zips, and customer info from Granot onto the Call Lead in Vantage. Booked Jobs (prior = 5) are skipped.

Auto-syncing

Actions

ScanAndSync (auto loop)

How ScanAndSync works (click to expand)

Every interval, the loop will:

  1. Re-scan the Granot Call Leads page (both Follow Up Estimates and Booked Jobs tables).
  2. For each row in Follow Up Estimates:
    • Send its phone, job_no, customer, zips, and est_cf to Vantage.
    • Vantage looks up a Call Lead by phone number.
    • If found and the fields differ, Vantage updates the Call Lead.
  3. Rows in Booked Jobs (prior = 5) are never touched.
  4. Rows in Follow Up Estimates with no matching Call Lead are reported as no_match and skipped — try again later once the CSR creates one.
  5. Errors do not stop the loop — they appear in the cycle history below.
⚠ Manual selection is paused while ScanAndSync is running. Stop the loop above to scan and sync rows by hand again.
